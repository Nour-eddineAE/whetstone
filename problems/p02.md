hint_pyspark: e.join(d, e.dept == d.dept_name, how="left") keeps every employee; unmatched department cols come back null.

**Result:** every employee together with the id of their department.

When the department isn't listed in the departments table, the id is empty (NULL) - but the employee still appears.
