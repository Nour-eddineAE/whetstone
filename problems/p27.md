hint: Correlated subquery - WHERE salary > (SELECT AVG(salary) FROM employees d WHERE d.dept = e.dept). Spark - AVG window over dept.
hint_pyspark: F.avg("salary").over(Window.partitionBy("dept")) as a column, then .where(salary > that dept average).
---
**Result:** the employees who earn more than the average salary **of their own department**.
