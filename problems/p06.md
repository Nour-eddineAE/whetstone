columns: dept, emp_id, name, salary
tables: employees
hint: ROW_NUMBER() OVER (PARTITION BY dept ORDER BY salary DESC, emp_id) then keep rn = 1.
---
**Result:** the single top earner in each department - one row per department.

If two employees tie for the top salary, the one with the smaller `emp_id` wins.
